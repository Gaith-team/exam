Nursing Informatics Competencies — full summary

Levels of competency
🟢 Entry-level nurse
  • Basic computer applications
  • Accurate documentation in EHR
  • Confidentiality & data security
🔵 Experienced nurse
  • Clinical info systems for decisions
  • Analyzes patient data trends
  • System evaluation & improvement
🟣 Informatics specialist
  • Designs & evaluates info systems
  • Leads training & change management
  • Research, policy & optimization
7 Core competency domains (اضغط على أي domain لتشوف تفاصيله)
1 Computer literacy
  • Basic hardware & software operations
  • Word processing, spreadsheets, presentations
  • Internet & email navigation
  • Cybersecurity basics (passwords, data protection)
Enables interaction with health info systems & digital documentation.
2 Information literacy
  • Identify information needs
  • Search electronic databases & digital libraries
  • Evaluate credibility & relevance of sources
  • Apply research findings to practice
Supports evidence-based nursing & lifelong learning.
3 Informatics literacy
  • DIKW framework (Data → Info → Knowledge → Wisdom)
  • Using electronic health records (EHRs)
  • Interpret clinical data & decision support alerts
  • Apply standardized nursing terminologies
Transforms raw data into meaningful clinical decisions.
4 Clinical informatics
  • Accurate electronic nursing documentation
  • Use of clinical decision support systems
  • Monitor patient outcomes digitally
  • Report & manage data for quality improvement
Improves patient safety, continuity & care coordination.
5 Management & leadership
  • Staffing, scheduling & budgeting systems
  • Analyze quality indicators & performance metrics
  • Support system implementation & evaluation
  • Lead technological change in organizations
Enhances organizational efficiency & strategic decision-making.
6 Ethical & legal
  • Maintain patient privacy & confidentiality
  • Understand data protection laws
  • Ethical use of patient data & digital records
  • Accountability in electronic documentation
Protects patient rights & maintains professional integrity.
7 Research & education
  • Use data for research & quality improvement
  • Support data collection & analysis
  • Integrate technology into nursing education
  • Evaluate educational technologies & learning systems
Promotes knowledge generation & continuous professional development.
Barriers & strategies
⚠ Barriers
  • Lack of training & educational resources
  • Resistance to change
  • Limited access to technology
  • Inadequate organizational support
  • High workload & time constraints
✓ Strategies
  • Integrate informatics into curricula
  • Continuous professional training
  • Organizational support & leadership
  • Hands-on practice with health info systems
  • Encourage lifelong learning & adaptability
